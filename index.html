AI is Everywhere!

From the videos YouTube recommends, to how Netflix knows what you'll like, to self-driving cars - AI is everywhere! Learning about AI helps you understand the world around you.

AI isn't just something scientists use - YOU can use it to make art, write stories, compose music, build games, and solve problems you care about!
